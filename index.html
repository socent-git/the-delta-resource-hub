Our thesis

Closing the gap between idea and scale.

"Many of India's most promising livelihood solutions struggle to reach scale, not because the ideas lack merit, but because the right capital, support, and ecosystem often don't exist in the right form, at the right stage."
01
Patient, catalytic capital
Through grants, equity, convertible structures, quasi-equity, and debt — designed to respond flexibly to the needs of early-stage ventures.
02
Thematic depth and clarity
A sharp focus on resilient livelihoods, with engagement at the intersections of climate, education, and health where they materially influence livelihood outcomes.
03
A high bar, intentionally
We back the right organisations, not the most organisations — prioritising founders with strong execution capability and the ambition to build durable, scalable institutions.
